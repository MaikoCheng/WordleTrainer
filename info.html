How to play?


Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word. You can go back after making a mistake.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


How to win?


You win after getting the entire word correctly.


How many words are there?


There are over 12,000 words in the game.
